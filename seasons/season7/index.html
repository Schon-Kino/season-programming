Season 7: GLEANING

It's the season of harvest and reflection: when we gather what remains, notice what has been lost, and prepare for what will return. We look at what was overlooked and say "there is still beauty here". This is a cinema of gleaning: of gathering fragments of life, grace, and feeling before the year exhales.
