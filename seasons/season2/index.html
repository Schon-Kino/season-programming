Season 2: FLIRTING

In this season we will explore the ways we connect and disconnect with each another. On tangled webs of people and emotions, flirting is all around — from Claire’s very particular knee to soviet Ninotchka falling hard for the charms of capitalism. What’s in a brief exchange of looks? Is flirting just innocent play? Or can the aftermath haunt us?
